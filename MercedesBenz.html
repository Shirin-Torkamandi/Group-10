Did You Know?

  • Mercedes-Benz was founded in 1926.
  • The brand is known for its luxury vehicles and innovative technology.
  • Mercedes-Benz produced the world's first gasoline-powered automobile in 1886.
  • The iconic Mercedes-Benz logo represents the brand's domination of land, sea, and air transportation.
  • The "Mercedes" name comes from a girl's name of Spanish origin meaning "grace" or "mercy."
  • Mercedes-Benz vehicles are often associated with high levels of comfort and safety.
  • The company has a history of motorsports success, including Formula One championships.
  • Mercedes-Benz has a strong focus on environmental sustainability and produces electric and hybrid vehicles.
